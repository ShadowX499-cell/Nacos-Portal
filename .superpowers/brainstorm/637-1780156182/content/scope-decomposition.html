Sub-project F — Super Admin RBAC & Audit Logs

3 distinct super-admin roles: Course Adviser, Head of Department, Result/Exam Officer — each with different permissions · Powerful Audit Log viewer — filterable by actor, role, action, date range, entity type; shows before/after diffs · Role-gated UI sections

D

Sub-project G — Revenue Dashboard

Clicking Total Revenue KPI card → dedicated revenue page · Breakdown by payment type (school fees, NACOS dues, result subscription) · Filters: date range, session, semester, program, level, payment type · Per-student payment history, downloadable reports

Also included in every sub-project

Recommended order

E → F → D → G — Start with profile/registration (foundational data), then lock down roles/permissions before building features that depend on them, then academic enrollment, then revenue analytics last.
